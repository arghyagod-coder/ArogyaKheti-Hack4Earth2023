At ArogyaKheti, we believe that the key to a bountiful harvest begins with understanding the unique needs of your soil. Our Fertilizer Recommendation System is your trusted ally in this journey, meticulously designed to ensure that your crops receive the precise nutrients they crave for optimal growth.

Unlocking the Power of Soil Knowledge:

Our Fertilizer Recommendation System is rooted in the science of precision agriculture. It all starts with your soil, the very foundation of your farming success. To get the most out of our system, you'll want to have your soil test report ready. If you haven't had one done yet, we'll show you how.

We firmly believe that understanding your soil's health is the first step towards a bountiful harvest. Soil tests provide invaluable insights into nutrient levels, pH balance, and soil texture, guiding you on the most effective way to nurture your crops. If you haven't conducted a soil test yet, don't worry; we're here to guide you through the process.

How to Get Your Soil Test Done:

  1. Sample Collection: Collect soil samples from various parts of your field, following our guidelines. Composite samples represent your entire plot accurately.
  2. Submit Your Samples: Reach out to your nearest agricultural department, agricultural university, Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK), or a reputable private soil testing laboratory. They will guide you on the submission process.
  3. Analysis and Report: Once your soil samples are analyzed, you will receive a comprehensive soil test report. This report is the key to unlocking the full potential of your land.
